## 2.2 FEATURE PRIORITIZATION RATIONALE

### Phase 1: Why Foundation First?
Enterprise buyers evaluate software on trust, not just features:
- **SOC 2 is table stakes** for 95% of enterprise deals
- **Mobile is non-negotiable** (70% of drivers use mobile first)
- **API is a gating factor** (customers have existing systems to integrate)

These are not features; they are requirements to play in the enterprise market.

### Phase 2: Why These Features?
These directly address competitive weaknesses:
- **AI/Predictive Maintenance** → Samsara's #1 differentiator
- **Route Optimization** → Geotab's core strength
- **Integrations** → Market requirement #1 from customer feedback
- **White-label** → Opens partnership revenue channel

These features are already in Samsara/Geotab. We're catching up.

### Phase 3: Why Specialization?
These create defensible competitive positions:
- **Autonomous dispatch** → Only Samsara/Motive offer (and not well)
- **AR capabilities** → Competitors don't offer (too new)
- **Compliance tracking** → Valued highly by enterprise buyers
- **Vertical solutions** → Hard for big players to optimize for niche
- **Global expansion** → Opens $15B+ international market

This is where we differentiate beyond catching up.
