================================================================================
TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS
================================================================================

Video Format:
- Resolution: 1080 x 1920 (vertical, 9:16 aspect ratio)
- Frame Rate: 30 fps
- Codec: H.264
- Bitrate: 8-10 Mbps
- File Size: < 500 MB
- Duration: Exactly 30 seconds (or 15 seconds alternative)

Audio:
- Format: AAC
- Sample Rate: 48 kHz
- Bitrate: 192 kbps
- Channels: Stereo
- Voiceover: Optional (works with music only)
- Volume: Background music at 60%, voiceover at 100%

Text Overlays:
- Font: SF Pro Display (Apple's system font)
- Title Text: Bold, 72pt
- Body Text: Medium, 48pt
- Color: White with 50% black shadow
- Animation: Fade in (0.3s), Hold (2s), Fade out (0.3s)
- Position: Top third and bottom third (safe area)

Transitions:
- Style: Smooth crossfade (0.3 seconds)
- No jarring cuts
- Maintain visual flow

Capture Settings:
- Use iPhone 15 Pro Max simulator (6.7")
- Record at 1x speed (real-time)
- Hide status bar or use clean status bar (10:30 AM, full battery)
- Ensure smooth animations
- No loading states visible


================================================================================
PRODUCTION NOTES
================================================================================

Recording App Screens:
1. Use Xcode Simulator's built-in screen recording
2. Or use QuickTime Player's screen recording feature
3. Ensure demo data is loaded (professional, not "test" names)
4. Record at 2x resolution, downscale in post for quality

Editing Software Recommendations:
- Final Cut Pro (Mac) - Professional
- iMovie (Mac) - Free, simple
- Adobe Premiere Pro - Cross-platform
- DaVinci Resolve - Free, powerful

Stock Footage (0:00-0:03 section):
- Shutterstock, iStock, or Pexels (free)
- Search terms: "paperwork frustration", "office documents", "manual tracking"
- Ensure commercial license if using free sources

Music Recommendations:
- Epidemic Sound (subscription)
- AudioJungle (one-time purchase)
- YouTube Audio Library (free)
- Apple Music library (check licensing)
- Suggested tracks: Upbeat, modern, professional, no lyrics

Voiceover:
- Optional (video works without voiceover)
- If using: Professional voice actor recommended
- Voice.com or Fiverr for affordable voiceover talent
- Male or female voice (test both, typically male for enterprise/government)
- Tone: Authoritative, trustworthy, clear

Color Grading:
- Maintain app's actual colors (don't over-saturate)
- Ensure readability of text
- Consistent brightness throughout
- Professional, not overly stylized

================================================================================
UPLOAD INSTRUCTIONS
================================================================================

App Store Connect Upload:
1. Log in to App Store Connect
2. Go to your app > App Preview and Screenshots
3. Select device size (6.7", 6.5", 5.5")
4. Click "+" to add app preview
5. Upload video file (H.264, 1080x1920, <500MB)
6. Add poster frame (frame shown before video plays)
7. Verify video plays correctly
8. Save changes

Recommended Poster Frame:
- Choose frame at 0:06 (app reveal) or 0:10 (GPS tracking)
- Should be visually appealing and clear
- Represents app well
- Compels user to press play

Video Position:
- Place video FIRST in screenshot gallery
- Video autoplays when user views product page
- Screenshots appear after video
- Users can swipe through screenshots without watching full video
