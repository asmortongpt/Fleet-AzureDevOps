## Key Features

### Real-Time Fleet Intelligence
- **Live GPS Tracking**: Monitor vehicle locations with 5-meter accuracy and route visualization
- **OBD2 Diagnostics**: Real-time engine health monitoring with 22 vehicle parameters
- **Fleet Dashboard**: At-a-glance metrics showing vehicle status, trips, maintenance, and fuel consumption
- **Instant Alerts**: Push notifications for maintenance needs, inspections, and vehicle issues

### Comprehensive Vehicle Management
- **Complete Vehicle Profiles**: Track make, model, VIN, mileage, fuel type, and status
- **23-Point Inspection System**: Digital inspection checklist with photo documentation
- **Maintenance Tracking**: Automated scheduling, service history, and reminder notifications
- **Document Management**: Scan and store vehicle registration, insurance, and service records

### Advanced Trip Tracking
- **Automatic Trip Recording**: GPS-based route capture with start/end times and mileage
- **Route Playback**: Visualize trip history with interactive map replay
- **Trip Analytics**: Fuel efficiency, idle time, distance traveled, and driver behavior metrics
- **Offline Synchronization**: Automatic data sync when connectivity is restored

### Professional Photo Capture
- **8 Photo Types**: Damage, inspection, VIN, odometer, maintenance, interior, exterior, general
- **Barcode & VIN Scanner**: 13 barcode formats including QR codes and VIN validation
- **Document Scanning**: Edge detection, perspective correction, and OCR-ready processing
- **Cloud Storage Integration**: Automatic upload to Azure Blob Storage with compression

### Enterprise Security
- **Multi-Factor Authentication**: OAuth 2.0, Face ID, Touch ID, and biometric security
- **AES-256 Encryption**: Military-grade encryption for data at rest and in transit
- **Certificate Pinning**: SHA-256 SSL/TLS certificate validation prevents MITM attacks
- **Jailbreak Detection**: Automatic security validation and policy enforcement
