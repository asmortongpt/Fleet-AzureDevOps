## FAQ Section

### Headline
## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q1: What size fleets is DCF Fleet Management designed for?
**A**: DCF Fleet Management scales from 25 vehicles to 5,000+ vehicles. We serve small county governments with 50-100 vehicles as well as large state agencies with 500+ vehicles. Our pricing and implementation approach adapts to your fleet size.

---

### Q2: How long does implementation take?
**A**: Typical implementation takes 4-6 weeks from contract signing to full deployment. This includes configuration, data migration, hardware installation, training, and go-live support. Large fleets (500+ vehicles) may take 8-12 weeks due to the phased rollout approach.

---

### Q3: What if we don't have reliable cellular coverage?
**A**: DCF Fleet Management works fully offline with automatic synchronization when connectivity is restored. This is a core design principle because government fleets often operate in rural areas with limited coverage. All features work offline including inspections, photo capture, trip tracking, and vehicle record access.

---

### Q4: Is this secure enough for government use?
**A**: Yes. DCF Fleet Management meets all federal security requirements including FISMA compliance, SOC 2 Type II certification, FIPS 140-2 Level 2 encryption, and NIST SP 800-175B cryptography standards. We're already deployed at federal, state, and local government agencies. We also support Azure Government Cloud for enhanced security.

---

### Q5: What devices are supported?
**A**: DCF Fleet Management is a native iOS app supporting iPhone and iPad running iOS 15.0 or later. Android support is planned for Q3 2026. Organizations can use employee-owned devices (BYOD) or provide organization-owned devices. We can assist with device procurement if needed.

---

### Q6: Can it integrate with our existing systems?
**A**: Yes. DCF Fleet Management integrates with Azure Active Directory for single sign-on, work order management systems, fuel card providers, and enterprise asset management platforms. We provide a RESTful API for custom integrations. Our implementation team has experience integrating with most common government systems.

---

### Q7: What about training and support?
**A**: Comprehensive training is included in implementation. We provide on-site or virtual training sessions, video tutorials, user guides, and quick reference cards. After deployment, you have 24/7 technical support via phone and email with average 2-hour response time. We conduct monthly check-ins for the first 3 months and quarterly business reviews ongoing.

---

### Q8: What is the typical ROI and payback period?
**A**: Based on actual customer results, organizations achieve 400-700% ROI in the first year with payback periods of 2-6 months. The primary driver is 30-35% reduction in maintenance costs through predictive maintenance and breakdown prevention. Additional savings come from administrative efficiency, fuel optimization, and fleet right-sizing.

---

### Q9: What happens if our drivers don't adopt the technology?
**A**: User adoption rate exceeds 95% because the app is intuitive and actually makes drivers' jobs easier. Inspections take 2 minutes instead of 10 minutes with paper. Reporting issues is instant with photo capture. The app works reliably offline. We've found that even skeptical veteran drivers become advocates after using it for a week.

---

### Q10: Is there a minimum contract term?
**A**: Standard enterprise contracts are annual with automatic renewal. We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee if you're not satisfied (though 99% of customers continue). Government agencies can often use shorter initial terms (6 months) to align with fiscal year budgets.

---

### Q11: How do you protect our data?
**A**: Your data is encrypted with AES-256 at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit. Data is stored in Azure cloud with enterprise-grade security, redundancy, and backup. You own your data and can export it at any time. We comply with GDPR and CCPA privacy regulations. We never sell or share your data with third parties.

---

### Q12: What about offline data limits?
**A**: There are no practical limits on offline data storage. The app stores all vehicle records, inspection history, and recent trip data locally on the device. A typical vehicle history uses about 5-10 MB. Even with hundreds of vehicles, total storage is well under 1 GB.

---

### Q13: Can we customize inspection checklists?
**A**: Yes. Inspection checklists are fully customizable to match your organization's requirements, DOT regulations, or manufacturer recommendations. You can create different checklists for different vehicle types (police cruisers, snowplows, sedans, etc.). Changes can be made anytime through the admin portal.

---

### Q14: What is the OBD2 hardware cost?
**A**: OBD2 adapters are $150 per vehicle (one-time cost). Installation is plug-and-play taking 30 seconds per vehicle. The adapters are Bluetooth-enabled, battery-powered (no vehicle battery drain), and compatible with all 1996+ vehicles. Adapters are included in pilot programs at no charge.

---

### Q15: How often is the app updated?
**A**: We release major updates 4-6 times per year with new features, enhancements, and security patches. Updates are automatic via the App Store. Critical security updates are deployed immediately. Customer feedback drives our product roadmap, and many requested features are prioritized for development.
