## Email Design Guidelines

### Visual Branding
- **Color Scheme**: Primary blue (#0066CC), secondary gray (#333333), accent green (#00CC66)
- **Logo**: Capital Tech Alliance logo at top of email
- **Font**: Clean, professional sans-serif (Arial, Helvetica, or similar)
- **Button Style**: Rounded corners, primary color background, white text

### Email Structure
- **Width**: Maximum 600px for optimal mobile rendering
- **Responsive**: Mobile-optimized for iPhone/Android
- **Whitespace**: Generous spacing between sections
- **Images**: Use sparingly, always with alt text
- **CTA Buttons**: Clear, actionable, prominent placement

### Best Practices
- **Subject Lines**: 40-50 characters for mobile preview
- **Preview Text**: 90-100 characters, compelling hook
- **Personalization**: Always use [First Name] variable
- **Segmentation**: Tailor content to audience (customers vs. prospects)
- **Testing**: A/B test subject lines and CTAs
- **Tracking**: Track opens, clicks, conversions

### Legal Requirements
- **Unsubscribe Link**: Visible at bottom of every email
- **Physical Address**: Company address in footer
- **CAN-SPAM Compliance**: Follow federal regulations
- **GDPR Compliance**: Follow EU data protection rules (if applicable)
