## üö® RISK MITIGATION

### Top 5 Risks & Mitigation Plans

**1. Hiring Delays (Probability: 40%)**
- **Impact:** 2-4 week timeline delay
- **Mitigation:** Start hiring Week 1, use contractors as backup
- **Contingency:** Offshore development team (Ukraine/Poland)

**2. GPS Integration Complexity (Probability: 30%)**
- **Impact:** Phase 1 delay or feature reduction
- **Mitigation:** Hire GPS/telematics expert consultant
- **Contingency:** Use Geotab/Samsara APIs instead of custom build

**3. SOC 2 Audit Delays (Probability: 25%)**
- **Impact:** Enterprise sales blocked until complete
- **Mitigation:** Start Week 1, hire compliance consultant
- **Contingency:** Pursue SMB customers (no SOC 2 required)

**4. Customer Acquisition Slower Than Expected (Probability: 35%)**
- **Impact:** Revenue targets missed by 20-40%
- **Mitigation:** Aggressive outbound sales, partnerships, channel sales
- **Contingency:** Extend runway with bridge financing

**5. Technical Debt Accumulation (Probability: 20%)**
- **Impact:** Phase 2-3 delays, quality issues
- **Mitigation:** Maintain 80% test coverage, weekly code reviews
- **Contingency:** Dedicated refactoring sprints

## üìû ESCALATION PATHS

### When Things Go Wrong

**Engineering Delays (>1 week behind schedule)**
- Week 1-4: VP Product + CTO daily standup
- Week 5+: Add contractors, reduce scope, extend timeline

**Hiring Failures (No candidates after 3 weeks)**
- Increase compensation 10-15%
- Engage executive recruiter
- Consider offshore/remote candidates

**Budget Overruns (>10% over plan)**
- Weekly finance review with CFO
- Freeze non-essential spending
- Reprioritize features to reduce scope

**Customer Pipeline Drying Up**
- CEO/VP Sales: Personal outreach to network
- Attend 2-3 industry events
- Launch referral program (10% commission)

**Technical Blockers (Showstopper bugs/issues)**
- All-hands war room until resolved
- Bring in external experts/consultants
- Communicate delays to customers proactively
