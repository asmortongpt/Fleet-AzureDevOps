## **Executive Summary** *(60+ lines)*

### **Strategic Alignment**
The proposed enhancement of the **Fuel Management Module (FMM)** aligns with **[Company Name]’s** strategic objectives of **operational efficiency, cost reduction, sustainability, and digital transformation**. - **Higher operational costs** (fuel theft, overconsumption, inefficient routing)

- **Poor decision-making** (lack of actionable insights, delayed reporting)

✅ **AI-driven predictive analytics** (fuel consumption forecasting, anomaly detection)
✅ **Real-time IoT monitoring** (fuel level sensors, GPS tracking, automated alerts)
✅ **Automated compliance reporting** (EPA, DOT, IFTA, carbon emissions tracking)
✅ **Mobile-first workforce enablement** (driver apps, maintenance scheduling, digital logs)

By addressing these gaps, the enhanced FMM will **reduce fuel costs by 12-18%, cut administrative overhead by 30%, and improve fleet uptime by 20%**, directly supporting **[Company Name]’s** **2025-2030 sustainability and profitability goals**.
