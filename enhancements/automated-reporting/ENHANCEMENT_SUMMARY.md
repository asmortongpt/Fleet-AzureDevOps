## **PROPOSED ENHANCEMENTS**
### **Detailed Feature List & Business Value**

| **Enhancement** | **Description** | **Business Value** | **KPI Impact** |
|----------------|----------------|-------------------|----------------|
| **1. AI-Powered Report Generation** | Natural language processing (NLP) for ad-hoc report requests (e.g., "Show me fuel efficiency trends for Q2") | Reduces report generation time by **90%** | ⬇️ Report time (5-7 days → <1 hour) |
| **2. Real-Time Dashboards** | Live fleet performance metrics (fuel, maintenance, driver behavior) with drill-down capabilities | Enables **proactive fleet management** (e.g., predictive maintenance) | ⬆️ Fleet uptime (5% improvement) |
| **3. Self-Service Analytics** | Drag-and-drop report builder with 50+ pre-built templates | Reduces IT dependency, **increases tenant satisfaction** | ⬆️ NPS (68 → 85+) |
| **4. Automated Data Validation** | AI-driven anomaly detection (e.g., fuel theft, odometer tampering) | **99.9% data accuracy**, reduces fraud | ⬇️ Compliance risks ($300K/year savings) |

| **7. API & Third-Party Integrations** | Seamless connectivity with ERP, telematics, and fuel card systems | **Eliminates data silos**, improves efficiency | ⬇️ Integration costs ($150K/year savings) |
| **8. Predictive Analytics** | ML models for fuel efficiency, maintenance forecasting, and route optimization | **10% cost savings** in fuel and maintenance | ⬇️ Operational costs ($500K/year) |
| **9. Mobile Reporting** | iOS/Android app for on-the-go report access | **Improves driver/manager productivity** | ⬆️ Operational efficiency (8% improvement) |
| **10. Audit & Compliance Tracking** | Automated logs for DOT, IFTA, and ELD compliance | **Reduces audit failures** | ⬇️ Compliance penalties ($200K/year) |
