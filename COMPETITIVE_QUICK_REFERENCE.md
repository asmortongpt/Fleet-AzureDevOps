**Response:** "True. Most customers use 10-15 of those integrations actively. We cover the 10-15 that matter. The other 400 are nice-to-have. We're also adding integrations; what's your #1 must-have integration?"

**Proof:** Feature/integration usage audit for similar customer. Show that 90%+ of Geotab customers use <20 of the 430.

### "Your company is too new"
**Response:** "Fair question. Our team has [X years] combined experience. We're funded by [investors]. And frankly, new means better technology and support. Geotab's platform is 15 years old."

**Proof:** Team bios, investor backing, product demo showing modern tech. Customer satisfaction metrics proving support quality.

---

### "Motive is cheaper"
**Response:** "For small fleets, yes. At your scale (150+ vehicles), you need enterprise features Motive doesn't offer. You've outgrown Motive. We're the natural next step."

**Proof:** Feature comparison chart showing advanced reporting, maintenance management, EV features. Customer story of company that grew out of Motive.

---

### "Verizon's network integration is important"
**Response:** "Understood. We integrate with Verizon services. You get the best of both: their network + our better interface/pricing."

**Proof:** Integration documentation. Demo showing Verizon API connection.

---

**Response:** "That's the risk with most vendors (Samsara = 90+ days, Geotab = 120+ days). We guarantee 6-week implementation or you don't pay. It's in our SLA."

**Proof:** Implementation SLA document. Customer case studies showing 6-week go-live.
