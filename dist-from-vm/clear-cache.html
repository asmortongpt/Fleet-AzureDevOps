Clear Cache & Fix White Screen

If you're experiencing a white screen or the app won't load, this tool will clear all cached data and reload the latest version.

Why you might need this:

After a deployment, your browser may have cached old files that no longer exist. This causes the app to fail loading.

What this will do:

1. Unregister the service worker
2. Clear all application caches
3. Clear browser storage
4. Reload the page with fresh content
