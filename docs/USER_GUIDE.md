## System Overview

### What Does This System Do?

The Fleet Management System helps you:

- **Track Vehicles** - See where your vehicles are in real-time with GPS tracking
- **Manage Maintenance** - Schedule service, track repairs, and prevent breakdowns
- **Monitor Drivers** - View driver performance, safety scores, and compliance
- **Control Costs** - Track fuel consumption, maintenance expenses, and reimbursements
- **Optimize Operations** - Plan routes, dispatch vehicles, and analyze fleet performance
- **Stay Compliant** - Manage safety forms (OSHA), inspections, and documentation
- **Communicate** - Built-in messaging, email, and Microsoft Teams integration

### Who Uses This System?

- **Fleet Managers** - Oversee entire fleet operations
- **Dispatchers** - Assign vehicles and monitor live locations
- **Maintenance Supervisors** - Schedule and track service work
- **Drivers** - Log trips, report issues, and view assignments
- **Administrators** - Configure system settings and manage users
- **Executives** - View high-level analytics and reports

## Getting Started

### First-Time Login

**Step 1: Open Your Browser**
- Navigate to: `https://fleet.capitaltechalliance.com` (or your organization's custom URL)
- Supported browsers: Chrome, Edge, Firefox, Safari (latest versions)

**Step 2: Choose Your Login Method**

You have two options:

#### Option A: Microsoft Single Sign-On (SSO) - Recommended
1. Click the **"Sign in with Microsoft"** button
2. You'll be redirected to Microsoft's login page
3. Enter your work email and password
4. Approve any multi-factor authentication (MFA) prompts
5. You'll be automatically redirected back to the dashboard

**Why use Microsoft SSO?**
- One-click login using your existing work account
- More secure (leverages your organization's security policies)
- No need to remember another password

#### Option B: Email & Password
1. Enter your email address
2. Enter your password
3. Click **"Sign In"**

**First-time password setup:**
If this is your first login with email/password, your administrator will provide you with a temporary password. You'll be prompted to change it on first login.

**Forgot your password?**
Click "Forgot Password?" on the login page and follow the instructions to reset it.

### Demo Mode

Not ready to use your real data? The system includes a **Demo Mode** with sample vehicles, drivers, and data so you can explore features safely.

## Key Features

### 1. Live GPS Tracking

**What it does:** Shows real-time location of all vehicles on a map.

**How to use it:**
1. Click **"Live GPS Tracking"** in the sidebar
2. You'll see a map with markers for each vehicle
3. **Click any vehicle marker** to see:
   - Current location and speed
   - Driver information
   - Trip details
   - Recent stops

**Tips:**
- Use the **Layer Controls** to show/hide traffic, weather, geofences
- Click **"Satellite"** for satellite imagery
- Use **"Follow Mode"** to track a specific vehicle
- Set up **Geofence Alerts** to get notified when vehicles enter/exit areas

**Screenshots:** [Map view would be shown here with vehicle markers]

---

### 2. Vehicle Management

**What it does:** Centralized database of all fleet vehicles.

**How to use it:**
1. Navigate to **"Fleet Dashboard"** or **"Garage & Service"**
2. See a list or grid of all vehicles
3. Click any vehicle to view:
   - Basic info (make, model, year, VIN, license plate)
   - Current status (available, in use, maintenance)
   - Mileage and fuel level
   - Assigned driver
   - Maintenance history
   - Live telemetry (if OBD2-equipped)

**Common actions:**
- **Add New Vehicle**: Click "+ Add Vehicle" button, fill in details
- **Edit Vehicle**: Click vehicle, then "Edit Details"
- **Assign Driver**: Click "Assign" and select from driver list
- **Schedule Maintenance**: Click "Schedule Service"

---

### 3. Maintenance Management

**What it does:** Track service schedules, work orders, and repair history.

**How to use it:**
1. Go to **"Predictive Maintenance"** or **"Maintenance Calendar"**
2. View upcoming maintenance:
   - Oil changes
   - Inspections
   - Tire rotations
   - State inspections

**Creating a maintenance request:**
1. Click **"Maintenance Request"** in the sidebar
2. Select the vehicle
3. Describe the issue or service needed
4. Set priority (Low, Medium, High, Critical)
5. Assign to a technician (or leave unassigned)
6. Submit

**Tracking work orders:**
- View status: Scheduled → In Progress → Completed
- Add notes and photos
- Track parts used and labor hours
- Generate maintenance reports

---

### 4. Driver Management

**What it does:** Manage driver profiles, performance, and compliance.

**How to use it:**
1. Navigate to **"People Management"** or **"Driver Performance"**
2. View list of all drivers
3. Click a driver to see:
   - Contact information
   - License details and expiration
   - Assigned vehicle(s)
   - Trip history
   - Performance metrics (safety score, fuel efficiency)
   - Training certifications

**Driver Scorecard:**
- View overall driver safety rating
- See breakdown of incidents, speeding, harsh braking
- Compare drivers to fleet average
- Identify training opportunities

---

### 5. Fuel Management

**What it does:** Track fuel purchases, consumption, and costs.

**How to use it:**
1. Go to **"Fuel Management"**
2. View fuel data by vehicle or time period
3. See metrics:
   - Total fuel cost
   - Miles per gallon (MPG) by vehicle
   - Fuel efficiency trends
   - Top fuel consumers

**Recording fuel purchases:**
1. Click **"Add Fuel Entry"**
2. Select vehicle
3. Enter gallons, cost, odometer reading
4. Add receipt (optional: use **Receipt Processing** to auto-scan)
5. Save

3. Set parameters:

4. Click **"Optimize"**

## Mobile App Usage

### Getting the Mobile App

**Download links:**
- **iOS**: Search "Fleet Manager" in Apple App Store
- **Android**: Search "Fleet Manager" in Google Play Store

**First-time setup:**
1. Download and install the app
2. Open the app
3. Enter your organization's Fleet URL (same as web)
4. Log in with your Microsoft account or email/password
5. Grant permissions (Location, Camera, Notifications)

### Mobile App Features

#### For Drivers:

**1. Vehicle Inspection**
- Tap **"Pre-Trip Inspection"**
- Walk through checklist (lights, tires, brakes, fluids)
- Take photos of any issues
- Submit inspection (required before starting trip)

**2. Trip Logging**
- Tap **"Start Trip"**
- System automatically tracks:
  - Start/end time
  - Mileage
  - Route taken
  - Stops made
- Tap **"End Trip"** when complete
- Add notes (purpose of trip, passengers, etc.)

**3. Report Issues**
- Tap **"Report Issue"**
- Select issue type (mechanical, accident, other)
- Take photos
- Add description
- Submit (creates maintenance request or incident report)

**4. View Assignments**
- See today's scheduled trips and tasks
- View route details and navigation
- Mark tasks complete

**5. Navigation**
- Tap any destination to open in navigation app (Google Maps, Apple Maps, Waze)
- Follow optimized route
- Updates dispatch in real-time

#### For Supervisors:

**6. Fleet Overview**
- See all vehicles on map
- View vehicle status
- Track live trips
- Monitor alerts

**7. Approve Requests**
- Review and approve mileage reimbursements
- Approve maintenance requests
- Authorize personal use

### Mobile Tips

**Battery Saving:**
- The app uses GPS tracking which can drain battery
- Enable "Battery Saver Mode" in app settings for longer battery life
- Keep your device charged or use a car charger

**Offline Mode:**
- The app works offline and syncs when connection is restored
- Complete inspections and log trips even without signal

**Notifications:**
- Enable push notifications to receive:
  - Trip assignments
  - Route changes
  - Maintenance alerts
  - Emergency messages

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: Do I need special training to use this system?**
A: No! The system is designed to be intuitive. This guide and the built-in help should be sufficient. Your organization may offer optional training sessions.

**Q: Can I access the system from home?**
A: Yes, as long as you have internet access and proper permissions. Use the same URL and login credentials.

**Q: Does the system work on mobile devices?**
A: Yes! The web interface is mobile-responsive, and there's a dedicated mobile app for iOS and Android.

**Q: How often is data updated?**
A: GPS locations update every 30 seconds. Other data (fuel, maintenance) updates in real-time when entered.

---

### Login & Access

**Q: I forgot my password. What do I do?**
A: Click "Forgot Password?" on the login page. You'll receive a reset link via email.

**Q: Why can't I see certain modules?**
A: Your access is based on your role. Contact your administrator if you need access to additional features.

**Q: The Microsoft login isn't working. What should I try?**
A:
1. Make sure you're using your work Microsoft account (not personal)
2. Clear your browser cache and cookies
3. Try a different browser
4. Use email/password login as backup
5. Contact IT support if issue persists

---

### GPS Tracking

**Q: Why isn't my vehicle showing on the map?**
A: Common reasons:
- Vehicle may not have GPS device installed
- GPS device may be unpowered or disconnected
- Vehicle may be in an area with poor signal (underground parking, etc.)
- Check with your fleet administrator

**Q: How accurate is the GPS tracking?**
A: Typically accurate to within 10-30 feet. Accuracy depends on GPS signal strength and environmental factors.

**Q: Can drivers see that they're being tracked?**
A: This depends on your organization's policies. Many organizations inform drivers that GPS tracking is in use for safety and efficiency purposes.

---

### Maintenance

**Q: How do I know when maintenance is due?**
A: The system sends alerts:
- Dashboard shows "Maintenance Due" badge
- Email notifications (if enabled)
- Mobile app push notifications
- Shows on vehicle detail page

**Q: Can I schedule maintenance for a specific date/time?**
A: Yes! Use the **Maintenance Calendar** to schedule service appointments. The system will reserve the vehicle for that time.

**Q: What if I need emergency maintenance?**
A: Use the **Maintenance Request** form and set priority to "Critical." This alerts the maintenance team immediately.

---

### Fuel Management

**Q: Do I need to manually enter fuel purchases?**
A: It depends on your setup:
- Manual entry: Yes, drivers or supervisors log fuel purchases
- Receipt scanning: Take a photo of the receipt, and the system auto-extracts data
- Fleet card integration: If your organization uses fleet fuel cards, purchases may auto-import

**Q: How is fuel efficiency calculated?**
A: MPG = (Miles Driven) / (Gallons Purchased)
The system tracks odometer readings and fuel purchases to calculate efficiency.

---

### Reports

**Q: Can I export reports to Excel?**
A: Yes! Most reports have an "Export" button with options for Excel, PDF, or CSV.

**Q: Can I schedule reports to run automatically?**
A: Yes! Use the **Custom Report Builder** to create a report, then click "Schedule" to set it to run daily, weekly, or monthly. You can have reports emailed to you.

**Q: How far back does historical data go?**
A: It depends on your organization's data retention policy. Typically, data is retained for several years.

---

### Mobile App

**Q: Does the mobile app use a lot of data?**
A: No, data usage is minimal. GPS tracking uses very little data. Maps are cached for offline use.

**Q: Will GPS tracking drain my phone battery?**
A: GPS does use battery, but the app is optimized to minimize impact. Enable "Battery Saver Mode" in settings for longer life.

**Q: Can I use the app offline?**
A: Yes! You can complete inspections, log trips, and record data offline. Everything syncs when you're back online.

---

### Troubleshooting

**Q: The page is loading slowly. What can I do?**
A: Try these steps:
1. Refresh the page (F5 or Ctrl+R)
2. Clear browser cache
3. Close other browser tabs
4. Check your internet connection
5. Try a different browser

**Q: I see an error message. What should I do?**
A:
1. Note the error message text
2. Try refreshing the page
3. If error persists, contact support with:
   - Screenshot of the error
   - What you were trying to do
   - Your browser and device type

**Q: Data isn't saving. Why?**
A:
- Check that all required fields are filled in (marked with *)
- Make sure you're clicking "Save" button
- Verify you have permission to edit that data
- Check your internet connection

For more troubleshooting help, see: [Troubleshooting Guide](TROUBLESHOOTING.md)
