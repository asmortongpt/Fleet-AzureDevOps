## SECTION 1: SALES PROCESS

### Seven-Stage Sales Methodology

#### **Stage 1: Prospecting**
- **Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)** - See Section 2
- **Target companies** by industry, fleet size, and geography
- **Lead sources**: LinkedIn, industry events, referrals, content marketing
- **Outreach templates**: Personalized cold email sequences, phone scripts

#### **Stage 2: Discovery (30-45 min)**
- **Discovery call script** - Establish rapport and gather information
- **Qualification questions (BANT)** - See Section 3
- **Pain point identification** - Map current challenges
- **Stakeholder mapping** - Identify buying committee members

#### **Stage 3: Demo (60 min)**
- **Demo script (3+ personas)** - See Section 4
- **Demo environment setup** - Live or sandbox environments
- **Objection handling** - See Section 5
- **Next steps** - Clear call-to-action and timeline

#### **Stage 4: Proof of Concept (2 weeks)**
- **POC scope template** - Define specific use cases to validate
- **Success criteria** - Measurable outcomes for POC period
- **Technical setup guide** - Integration and configuration steps
- **Evaluation framework** - How customer will assess success

#### **Stage 5: Proposal (1 week)**
- **Proposal template** - Customized per prospect needs
- **ROI calculator** - See Section 8
- **Pricing configurations** - Fleet size-based pricing tiers
- **Reference customers** - Similar company case studies

#### **Stage 6: Negotiation**
- **Pricing flexibility guidelines** - See Section 7
- **Contract terms** - Standard vs. custom terms
- **Legal review** - Internal and customer legal teams
- **Redline handling** - Process for managing contract changes

#### **Stage 7: Close**
- **Contract signing** - E-signature process
- **Handoff to Customer Success** - Transition documentation
- **Welcome email** - Set expectations for onboarding
- **Kickoff scheduling** - Implementation timeline and resources

### Sales Cycle Timeline
**Average Sales Cycle: 45-90 days**

## SECTION 2: IDEAL CUSTOMER PROFILE (ICP)

### Firmographics

**Industry Vertical:**
- Logistics and 3PL: 40%
- Construction and Heavy Equipment: 35%
- Transportation and Courier: 25%

**Fleet Characteristics:**
- Fleet Size: 50-250 vehicles (sweet spot), 25-500 acceptable
- Annual Revenue: $10M-$500M
- Employee Count: 50-2,000
- Geographic Focus: North America (Phase 1), Global expansion (Phase 4)

**Operational Maturity:**
- Established operations with growth plans
- Multi-location/multi-facility operations
- Growing vehicle utilization challenges

### Technographics

**Current Technology:**
- Legacy fleet management (Verizon, Geotab) or spreadsheet-based tracking
- Cloud-based office operations (Office 365, Salesforce)
- Mobile-first workforce culture
- Tech-forward management (willing to adopt SaaS)

**Integration Requirements:**
- ERP systems (SAP, NetSuite)
- Fuel card systems
- GPS/telematics devices
- Accounting software

### Psychographics

**Decision Drivers:**
- Cost-conscious (Samsara pricing considered prohibitive)
- Growth-focused (scaling fleet operations)
- Data-driven decision making
- Sustainability and environmental consciousness
- Operational efficiency as competitive advantage

### Buying Committee

**Economic Buyer:** CFO, VP Operations
- Focused on ROI, TCO, and budget impact

**Technical Buyer:** CTO, IT Director
- Concerned with security, compliance, integrations, infrastructure

**User Buyer:** Fleet Manager, Dispatcher, Maintenance Manager
- Day-to-day users, focused on usability and operational impact

**Coach:** Internal champion or power user
- Advocates for solution internally, understands pain points

**Influencer:** Driver feedback (for mobile/driver experience)
- Real-world usability feedback

## SECTION 3: DISCOVERY QUESTIONS

### Business Discovery Questions

1. **Fleet Composition** - How large is your fleet? (vehicles, drivers, facilities)
2. **Current Challenges** - What are your biggest fleet management challenges today?
3. **Current Solution** - What system do you currently use for fleet management?
4. **Satisfaction** - What do you like and dislike about your current system?
5. **Vision** - What would an ideal fleet management solution look like for you?
6. **Urgency** - What's driving this evaluation now? What's the business driver?
7. **Budget** - What budget do you have allocated for fleet management solutions?
8. **Timeline** - When do you need to make a decision?
9. **Decision Process** - Who else is involved in the decision-making process?
10. **Success Definition** - What does success look like in 12 months?

### Technical Discovery Questions

1. **Integrations** - What integrations do you need? (ERP, fuel cards, GPS, accounting)
2. **Compliance** - What compliance requirements must be met? (DOT, OSHA, EPA, FMCSA)
3. **Infrastructure** - What's your IT infrastructure? (cloud, on-premise, hybrid)
4. **Security** - What's your security posture? (SOC 2, ISO 27001, certifications required)
5. **Mobile** - Do you need mobile access for drivers/technicians? What devices?
6. **Data** - What data volume and historical data retention needed?
7. **Deployment** - Cloud-only or on-premise options required?
8. **Support** - What level of technical support do you require?

### BANT Qualification Criteria

- **Budget:** Minimum $50K annually (typically $30-150K range)
- **Authority:** Speaking directly with or having confirmation from decision maker
- **Need:** Clear, specific pain points with demonstrated urgency
- **Timeline:** Decision within 90 days from initial contact

## SECTION 5: OBJECTION HANDLING

### "Your pricing is too expensive"

**Root Cause:** Comparing against lower-tier competitors or legacy systems

**Response:**
- "I understand pricing is important. Let's do a true cost comparison"
- **Show TCO (Total Cost of Ownership):**
  - Fleet Platform: $45/vehicle/month = $54,000/year (100 vehicles)
  - Samsara: $55/vehicle/month = $66,000/year + $500 implementation
  - Hidden Samsara costs: 3-year contract locks, poor support, custom dev fees
  - Result: Fleet is 25-30% cheaper over 3 years

- **Use ROI Calculator:**
  - "Most customers see payback in 12-18 months through fuel optimization alone"
  - Show fuel savings: 8-12% reduction in fuel costs
  - Show maintenance savings: 15-20% reduction through predictive maintenance

- **Flexible Payment Options:**
  - Monthly billing for startups/uncertain buyers
  - Annual prepay: 10% discount
  - Multi-year: 15% discount Year 1, 10% Years 2-3
  - Volume discounts: 15-25% for 250+ vehicles

**Closing:** "Would a phased approach help? Start with core vehicles, expand as you see ROI?"

---

### "We're happy with our current solution [Samsara/Geotab/etc.]"

**Root Cause:** Inertia, switching costs, or lack of awareness of alternatives

**Response:**
- **Validate existing satisfaction:** "That's great - what's working well with [competitor]?"
  - Listen for what they value (can address in demo)

- **Identify pain points:** "What could they improve on?"
  - Common answers: "Too expensive," "Poor support," "Hard to use," "Limited reporting"

- **Create urgency:** "We're seeing customers make switches when they need to add 50+ vehicles. Is that something you're planning?"

- **Feature Comparison:**
  - Show feature parity matrix
  - Highlight unique features (AI-powered maintenance, carbon tracking, superior mobile)
  - Demo the areas where they complained about current provider

- **Proof Points:**
  - "Here are 5 customers who switched from [competitor] to us..."
  - Share testimonials and case studies
  - Offer reference calls with similar companies

- **Lower Switching Costs:**
  - "Our onboarding is 2 weeks vs. 6-8 weeks"
  - "We handle data migration"
  - "30-day free trial to validate before switching"

**Closing:** "Would a POC make sense to compare head-to-head with your current system?"

---

### "We need to see it work in our environment first"

**Root Cause:** Risk aversion, wanting proof before commitment

**Response:**
- **Offer validation options:**
  - 30-day free trial with your data
  - 2-week proof of concept (see Section 1, Stage 4)
  - Sandbox environment with sample data
  - API test environment for technical integration

- **Reduce risk:**
  - "Many companies start with a 30-day trial"
  - "We'll do data migration and setup for free during POC"
  - "You can assess yourself - we don't require a commitment"

- **Provide social proof:**
  - Case studies from similar companies (same industry/size)
  - Customer testimonials and references
  - Before/after metrics from case study customers

- **Accelerate proof:**
  - "Let's define success criteria together"
  - "Here are metrics similar companies measure on day 1, week 1, week 2"
  - "We'll provide weekly reviews"

**Closing:** "Let's schedule a 30-minute POC planning call - I'll walk you through what we'd accomplish in those 30 days."

---

### "We're not ready yet - check back in [6 months/next year]"

**Root Cause:** No immediate urgency, competing priorities, budget cycles

**Response:**
- **Understand timeline:**
  - "When you say 6 months, what needs to happen first?"
  - "Is that a budget cycle? Staffing decision? System migration?"
  - Get specific trigger event

- **Establish commitment:**
  - "Perfect. Let's put a specific date on the calendar"
  - "What should I send you to stay top-of-mind?"

- **Stay connected:**
  - Monthly check-in calls (not pushy, genuinely helpful)
  - Educational content: whitepapers, webinars, case studies
  - Industry reports and market insights
  - Quarterly business reviews

- **Create FOMO carefully:**
  - "Several companies in your industry implemented this Q4..."
  - "We're building this predictive feature - you mentioned wanting that"
  - "Budget fills up early - want me to reserve your Q2 slot?"

- **Add to nurture sequence:**
  - 5-email sequence over 6 months
  - Targeted content by persona
  - Value-focused, not sales-focused

**Closing:** "I'll put you on my calendar for [specific date] - let's aim for a full demo then. Sound good?"

  - "Actually, that's on our roadmap for [Q2/Q3]"

  - "In the meantime, here's how we can accomplish this with our current tools..."

  - "For enterprise customers with complex requirements, we offer custom dev"

  - "Our open API allows you to build custom features"

## SECTION 8: ROI CALCULATOR

### Framework and Methodology

**Savings Categories:**
1. Fleet management platform cost (vs. current spend)
2. Fuel consumption optimization
3. Maintenance cost reduction (preventive vs. reactive)
4. Administrative labor cost reduction
5. Accident/insurance cost reduction (optional)

### Input Variables

**Fleet Data:**
- Number of vehicles: [integer]
- Average annual miles per vehicle: [miles]
- Fleet average fuel consumption: [MPG]

**Current State Costs:**
- Current fleet management cost per vehicle: [$/month]
- Current fuel costs: [$/month total]
- Current maintenance costs: [$/month total]
- Administrative labor: [hours/week]
- Administrative labor hourly rate: [$/hour]
- Optional - Insurance costs: [$/month]

### Output Calculations

**Annual Current Costs:**
```
Fleet Management: X vehicles × $/month × 12 months
Fuel: Annual fuel spend
Maintenance: Annual maintenance spend
Admin Labor: Hours/week × 52 weeks × $/hour
Insurance: Annual insurance spend (optional)
Total Annual Cost: [Sum]
```

**Projected Annual Costs with Fleet:**
```
Fleet Management: X vehicles × Fleet pricing × 12 months
Fuel: Annual fuel spend × 0.92 (8% optimization)
Maintenance: Annual maintenance × 0.85 (15% reduction)
Admin Labor: Hours/week × 0.6 × 52 weeks × $/hour (40% reduction)
Insurance: Annual insurance × 0.98 (2% reduction optional)
Total Annual Cost: [Sum]
```

**ROI Calculations:**
```
Annual Savings: Current Cost - Fleet Cost
ROI %: (Annual Savings / Current Cost) × 100
Payback Period: (Fleet annual cost) / (Monthly Savings)
```

  - Discovery to Demo: 70%
  - Demo to POC: 60%
  - POC to Proposal: 80%
  - Proposal to Close: 50%

### Revenue Metrics (Lagging Indicators)

**Individual Contributor Metrics:**
- Quota per AE: $1M/year ($250K/quarter)
- Year-to-date revenue: Track monthly
- Quota attainment: Target 100%+ (120%+ bonus)
- Commission/bonus: Based on quota attainment

**Team Metrics:**
- Total team revenue: Sum of all AEs
- Team quota attainment: %
- Win rate: Deals won / deals closed
- Average contract value (ACV): Total revenue / number of deals
- Customer acquisition cost (CAC): Sales costs / new customers acquired
- CAC payback period: Months to recover CAC from customer revenue

**Customer Metrics:**
- Customer acquisition: New customers per month
- Gross margin: Revenue - COGS (support/onboarding)
- Gross margin %: (Revenue - COGS) / Revenue
- Churn rate: Customers lost / total customers (target <5% annually)
- Net revenue retention: (Starting revenue + expansion - churn) / starting revenue
